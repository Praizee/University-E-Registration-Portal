About the Portal

The Bells University E-Registration portal converts a general-purpose students registration activities to an electronic system that provides students with a speedy, efficient gateway to personal information and essential school services.

The portal is a secure website, designed for use by presently enrolled students of the University. You are expected to log in to the portal with your E-Registration card number and password.

Simplicity, ease of use, responsiveness and hitch-free service is all we intend to deliver with this new portal.

Instructions

  • As a returning student, Log-in with your matriculation number as username (e.g PAC/0123/UG/NSC/001), then enter the given default password (abcd1234).
  • As a newly admitted student, Log-in with your JAMB Number (e.g 65491684HH) then enter the given default password (abcd1234).

  • Note: You will be requested to change the default password to your own chosen password. After changing the password, you need to logout and re-login with the new password. Please keep your password safe. If you need assistance on your Reg No, JAMB No or On-line Application form Number, do not hesitate to contact the ICT Center or email: adeolaastephen@gmail.com
