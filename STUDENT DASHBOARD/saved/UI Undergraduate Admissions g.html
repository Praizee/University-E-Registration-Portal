Signing into the portal

Your Username is your Jamb Registration Number eg 44444444UI

Your Password is your Surname at first login e.g Adelopo

If you forgot your password, click the reset password link to reset the password

At first login, the system prompts you to change your password. The minimum length of your password is 6 characters

Keep your SECRET QUESTION; for it is used to reset your password to your SURNAME (in capital lettrs) in case you cannot remember it

Filling the Bio Data Form

There are seven sub-menus. Sub menu number one has been completed by JAMB. You are required to fill the following:
  • Personal Information
  • Next of Kin Information
  • O'Level Result(s) for DE only
  • Post Secondary Result for DE only
  • Fall Back Institution(s)
  • Attestation

NOTE: Do not use apostrophe(') in your data. It can give you unnecessry stress when filling the forms

Downloading Sample Questions

You can download questions for up to 4 subjects

Once you download a question on a subject, the subject will be locked down as one of your 4 subjects

Once you have downloaded sample questions from 4 subjects you will not be able to download sample questions from any other subject
